Tic Tac Toe

RULES

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid, making a total of 9 spaces.
  • Player 1 usually marks their spaces with "X," and Player 2 marks theirs with "O."
  • Players take turns placing their marks in empty spaces on the grid
  • The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game
